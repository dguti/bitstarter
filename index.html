Do you need to follow every day an official gazette to check as soon as possible if an information of your interest has been published?

After the accomplishment of this project, you would be able to receive in your email or smartphone very valuable hot alerts about whether a public examination has been called, a fine to you has been published or new laws will be in force.

Backers will receive in return a premiun account.

Free accounts will allow users access the service with limits on the number of alerts and gazettes. These users will receive some advertisments based on their alerts. They could only use the Android application for receiving alerts at most once a day.

Premium accounts will allow users access the service with enhanced features:

  • They will be able to program alerts in packs of ten (each pack needs a account upgrade).
  • They will be able to configure whether they want to receive advertisments or not.
  • They will be able to pull for alerts each 4 hours.

The project will be developed in several phases.

  • First, the alert engine will be programmed and tested against several online gazettes.
  • Second, a web page will be created in order to allow users registering, configuring their accounts, programming alerts and checking for notifications.
  • And third, an Android application will be developed. This application will pull for alerts, but for configuration purposes users will have to access the responsive web application.

The invested money will allow the development of the platform and one year of service.

After the project accomplisment more work will be done if enough users support it:

  • An iPhone application will be developed.
  • Enhanced services will be implemented: keyword suggestions, smarter alerts, more gazettes supported...

I'm looking forward for your support.

David.

FAQ

For what gazettes could alerts be programmed?
When backing this project for at least $10, you will be able to provide a list or gazettes of your interest. Only the 5 more popular will be considered. When backing this project for at least $100, your first suggested gazette will be always considered. It is a condition for all gazettes (independently of the backed amount) to be freely accessible and there must exist permalinks for each publication in a recognized format (txt or pdf).
Will the IPhone application be ready after the project accomplishment?
No, but the development will be afforced if enough users support the platform. Anyway you will always able to get your notifications by email or check them in the web.
